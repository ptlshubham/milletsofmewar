What is Millets?

Millets are cereal crops and small seed grasses, which are widely used in African and Asian countries. For ages millets were commonly used to feed birds and animals until the health benefits of these superfoods were known to the world. In fact, India is known as the second-largest diabetes capital of the world, and this has led to a surge in the demand for millets. Moreover, these gluten-free millets can play a pivotal role in preventing and curing several health issues. Millets are rich sources of fiber, minerals like magnesium, phosphorous, iron, calcium, zinc and potassium and can help cure several health issues.
